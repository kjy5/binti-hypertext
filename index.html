When people ask me where I come from, I have a hard time answering...

I grew up in many places:
California, Singapore, Minnesota, Ohio, and Washington.

Experiencing and expressing cultural differences is a very human situation. It’s very inherent in how we behave. Binti was experiencing others being unfamiliar with her culture. I experienced something like this before in middle school.

Binti is a science fiction novella by Nnedi Okorafor, which follows the story of a young girl named Binti from the Himba tribe in Namibia, who is the first of her people to be accepted into a prestigious intergalactic university. The novella explores themes of identity, culture, and tradition, as well as the challenges and rewards of pursuing knowledge and breaking down barriers between different worlds and ways of life.

Children often express themselves with no filter. I am Asian American, and less than 2% of the people in Duluth, Minnesota looked like me and enjoyed and same foods as me. So, when I brought my lunch to school, my classmates initially gawked at what I had and turned away in disgust. To them, traditional delicacies such as 咸鸭蛋 (salted duck egg) or 炸酱面 (noodles in fermented bean sauce) looked like rotten eggs and spaghetti so old that it turned black. We were all children then, and children learn fast. I soon became their friend, and I was able to share my culture with them.

A salted duck egg is a preserved duck egg that has been soaked in a brine solution containing salt and other ingredients such as charcoal and tea leaves. The resulting egg yolk is typically salty and savory, with a unique texture and flavor. It is a popular ingredient in various Asian cuisines, used in dishes such as mooncakes, steamed buns, and congee.

炸酱面 is a Chinese dish called "Zhajiangmian" in English, which consists of thick wheat noodles served with a savory sauce made from fermented soybeans, ground pork, and vegetables such as onions and cucumbers. It is a popular comfort food in northern China and can be found in many Chinese restaurants around the world.

Use the arrow keys to navigate or click on hyperlinked text.

A hypertext document is a type of electronic document that contains hyperlinks, which are clickable links that can take you to other parts of the document, or to other documents altogether. This allows for a non-linear way of reading and navigating through information, making it easier to access and connect related content. The World Wide Web is perhaps the most famous example of a hypertext document system, where web pages are linked together through hyperlinks.
